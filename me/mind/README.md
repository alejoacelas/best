<!--ai-->
- **It's preparation for a psychiatrist conversation, not a substitute.** The
  research exists so Alejo can walk in informed with concrete options — drug
  names, doses, side-effect numbers, the questions worth asking. Not medical
  advice, not self-prescription.
- **The objective is full-cycle functioning, not just symptom suppression** —
  preserve cognition, personality, and long-term productivity while reducing the
  cost of elevated states and depressive aftermaths. Standard trials often count
  valued hypomanic traits as "relapse"; the project uses a wider full-cycle
  accounting instead.
- **Distinguish evidence types.** Keep personal facts, self-reports, hypotheses,
  clinician advice, and source-backed literature separate. Flag where evidence is
  Bipolar I-heavy rather than Bipolar II-specific. Treat `claude-convos.txt` as
  context, not verified evidence.
- **Save the audit trail.** Formal research runs go in `investigations/<slug>/`;
  ad-hoc synthesis in `notes/YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md`.
