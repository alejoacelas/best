# My style

A read of Alejo's own presence, in the same terms as the four profiles next door.
Sourced from [myea.blog](https://myea.blog). Read the [charisma note](charisma.md) first;
this applies its map to me.

## Thesis

My style is **radical self-exposure in the service of care**. I lay my interior bare —
motives, crushes, screw-ups, unflattering math — and I do it *toward* people, to move
them. Warmth is the constant; candor is the engine; a hunt for hard rules is the shape
my thinking takes. The nearest of the four is McCartney (warmth + a real gift, worn
lightly), not Thiel — even though I borrow Thiel's provocations.

## The moves

- **Candor past the point of comfort.** The signature. I "persistently try finding ways
  to be more candid than I've been in the past" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)),
  and the stated rule is [be so candid they ask you to stop](https://myea.blog/so-candid) —
  "just keep going until people literally start asking you to stop." At dinner I'll ask
  "You seem good at spotting exceptional talent, what's your take on me?" This is what
  the charisma note calls *accessible vulnerability*: I manufacture, deliberately and to
  an extreme, the status-lowering openness that makes McCartney endearing. It's my
  strongest charisma lever and my biggest liability at once.
- **Provocative true wording.** I "find the most provocative true wording for what I
  think" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)) — "a bunch of you are
  absolute morons" ([violent-passion](https://myea.blog/violent-passion)), "may I steal
  this pen from you?" This is the Thiel move (say the disagreeable true thing), but I run
  it warm, as an invitation, not a verdict.
- **Rule-distillation.** I "find a small set of principles to follow religiously and then
  ignore most other considerations" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)):
  a meal is protein + carb + vegetable; [do things now](https://myea.blog/do-it-now);
  candor until they beg. When a trait misfires I don't resolve to try harder — I write a
  rule ([my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)). The essays are me building my own operating
  manual in public.
- **Confession as structure.** I narrate my own worst moments and leave the reasoning
  unresolved — the [schedule-send episode](https://myea.blog/my-bad), the [pride](https://myea.blog/pride)
  fueling me while a friend has just died. I show the working, including the parts that
  don't tidy up. This is PG's think-out-loud, pushed to the emotional register PG never
  enters.
- **Sharp care.** The care I want is "sharp, like a long stick or a sword, that can push
  through the crust and break things and shatter and reshape" ([caring](https://myea.blog/caring)) —
  opinionated, wanting to change you, the opposite of my grandparents' undirected
  blanket. Most of my warmth comes with a direction attached.
- **Direct address.** I write letters — to Robin ([what-i-want](https://myea.blog/what-i-want),
  [colourful-heart](https://myea.blog/colourful-heart)), to a future manager
  ([my-manager](https://myea.blog/my-manager)), to you. Intimate second person; the reader
  is a specific person I'm trying to reach.
- **Fiction as moral instrument.** Mother of Learning, HPMOR, Unsong, *Carrie*, the
  Giving Tree, Superman. I reason about how to live through stories and cite them like
  evidence.
- **Speed over polish.** Dictated, bullet-shaped, published as drafts. The style *is*
  unfinished on purpose — [do it now](https://myea.blog/do-it-now), iterate in public.

## Where it comes from

Candor is an overcorrection I turned into a method. In my own words: "I used to restrict
myself much, much more... candor is kind of like my overcorrection" ([avoidant](https://myea.blog/avoidant)).
I pair it with a stated **low cognitive empathy** — hard to read others' feelings — so I
lean on explicit rules and explicit disclosure *because* I can't reliably read the room.
That's the through-line: the rules, the candor, and the "harm only counts if you tell me
in words" stance are all the same adaptation to the same limitation, turned into a
recognizable voice.

## What it's for

- **Finding your people fast.** My blog's own aims: a long-term partner, collaborators,
  better conversations ([lay-bare](https://myea.blog/lay-bare)) — and by my account it's
  working. Candor is a filter: it repels the wrong people cheaply and bonds the right
  ones hard.
- **Attracting talent and mentors.** "An important mission pursued with conviction is one
  of the main factors for attracting talent. The other is being able to recognize the
  talent's strengths and create a narrative for how they fit" ([conviction](https://myea.blog/conviction)).
  The [manager post](https://myea.blog/my-manager) is this style used as a recruiting tool.
- **Moving people to act.** The day job is "proselytizing Claude Code" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits))
  and coaching friends toward ambition ([violent-passion](https://myea.blog/violent-passion)).
  The intensity is the point — it's how I raise other people's aspirations.
- **Building trust that compounds.** [You can't compound on your own](https://myea.blog/cant-own):
  most of my leverage is other people, and being unusually easy and vivid to know is how
  I plug into their networks.

## The costs (be honest)

- **The candor has a body count.** [My pattern of disregarding people's immediate
  preferences](https://myea.blog/my-bad) — the schedule-send — strained someone I love.
  Low cognitive empathy means I under-detect the harm my openness does, so my own alarm
  can't be trusted to stop me. The rule I landed on (default to their stated preference;
  override only after long thought) is the guardrail; the style needs it.
- **Provocation can read as contempt.** "Absolute morons" lands as bracing to some and as
  cruelty to others. Warmth has to be visibly present or the Thiel move curdles.
- **Earnestness with no irony is polarizing.** No cynical exit means people who don't want
  that intensity have nowhere to stand. That's fine as a filter; know it's a filter.
- **Speed costs compression.** [PG's density](people/paul-graham.md) is the thing my drafts
  most lack. Fast-and-bare is the voice; fast-and-*tight* would be strictly better.

## How to develop it

1. **Choose McCartney over Thiel, on purpose.** My gift is warmth, not menace. Keep the
   provocative true wording, but land it warm — the [Thiel profile](people/peter-thiel.md)
   shows what the cold version trades away, and it's not a trade I should want.
2. **Add PG's compression to the candor.** Same exposure, fewer words. Run drafts through
   the [density test](people/paul-graham.md) before publishing; the honesty survives
   cutting, the flab doesn't.
3. **Keep the guardrail explicit and public.** Candor without the [my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)
   rule is the failure mode. Commit the rules where others can see me break them — that's
   the candor pointed back at myself.
4. **Borrow Tyler's curiosity as the warm form of provocation.** [Cowen](people/tyler-cowen.md)
   changes people by asking the question only he'd ask. For someone with low cognitive
   empathy, *asking* ("what's your take on me?") outsources the read I can't do — it's
   already my instinct; make it the default over asserting.
5. **Name the vulnerability instead of only enacting it.** The endearing quadrant needs
   accessible vulnerability, not raw oversharing. "Here's what I want, you don't have to
   give it" (the Maya frame, [avoidant](https://myea.blog/avoidant)) is the calibrated
   version — costs nothing, still fully open.

## Map to the four

- **McCartney** — closest. Warmth as default, a real gift worn lightly, openness that
  lowers status distance. My endearing quality and his are the same mechanism.
- **Graham** — shared: essay-as-thinking-out-loud, admitting uncertainty to earn trust,
  concrete-first. Missing: his compression and his calm.
- **Cowen** — shared: prolific, associative, curious, links everywhere, quests-not-jobs
  range. Missing: his deadpan restraint (I run hot where he runs dry).
- **Thiel** — I borrow the contrarian provocation and definite optimism, but I'm his
  inverse on the axis that defines him: I badly want to be liked, and I lead with warmth.

## Open

- Which of these is actually load-bearing for the good I want to do, versus just *me*?
  Candor and sharp care feel core; the provocation might be optional.
- The [charisma note](charisma.md) says presence is the most learnable, highest-leverage
  component. I have warmth and power; **presence** (full, undivided attention on one
  person) is the piece my speed-and-fragmentation habit actively erodes. Likely the best
  single thing to train.
