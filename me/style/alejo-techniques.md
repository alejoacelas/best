# The techniques Alejo already runs

The [charisma note](charisma.md) lists techniques — Antonakis's twelve tactics, Cabane's
presence/warmth/power, the pratfall move. This maps them onto what Alejo *already does*,
quoted from [the blog](https://myea.blog). It's a mirror, not a drill: where he runs a
tactic without trying, where he over-runs it, and the one he skips by default. The point
isn't to start practicing — it's to recognize the ones already his so he leans on them on
purpose, and to catch the two or three he overfits.

## At a glance

| Technique | Does he run it? |
|---|---|
| Metaphor / analogy | Heavily — maybe too heavily |
| Stories / anecdotes | Heavily — the default unit of his writing |
| Contrasts | Heavily |
| Rhetorical questions | Heavily |
| Three-part lists | Sometimes — he tends to run past three |
| Moral conviction | Heavily — the engine |
| Reflecting the group's sentiment | Yes, usually to challenge it |
| High goals | Heavily |
| Confidence the goal is reachable | Yes, paired with the goals |
| Warmth signals (name, reflect, remember) | Heavily — the constant |
| Pratfall (one flaw on top of competence) | His signature — the one he most overfits |
| State / emotional contagion | Heavily, in both directions |
| Animated voice, facial expression | Can't read from text |
| Gesture | The solo high-five is a real one |
| Presence (full, undivided attention) | **Not by default — the gap** |

## The verbal tactics he runs without trying

**Metaphor and analogy.** Constant, and vivid. Care as two objects: "My grandparents'
care feels a bit like a blanket... The care I'm most excited by feels sharp, like a long
stick or a sword, that can push through the crust and break things and shatter and
reshape" ([caring](https://myea.blog/caring)). Career mobility as migration: "creating
your own personal country, connecting to the infrastructure that's built in there"
([cant-own](https://myea.blog/cant-own)). Whole posts are one extended analogy — the
time-loop in [inmortal](https://myea.blog/inmortal), the tree in
[giving-tree](https://myea.blog/giving-tree).
*Overfit watch:* he stacks them, and has caught himself doing it — "it's becoming kind of
impossible because I've already used that adjective for like maybe seven people so far"
([colourful-heart](https://myea.blog/colourful-heart)). One sharp image lands harder than
three softened ones.

**Stories.** The default unit. He argues by narrating: the [schedule-send
episode](https://myea.blog/my-bad), the stubbed toe and "I think I resent you"
([avoidant](https://myea.blog/avoidant)), the dinner blurts
([so-candid](https://myea.blog/so-candid)). He rarely asserts a principle without a scene
under it. This is the most reliably charismatic thing he does and the least at risk of
overfitting.

**Contrasts.** He thinks in opposed pairs, often in the title:
[Stop feeling dread. Start feeling regret](https://myea.blog/stop-dread) — "Dread is
undirected... Regret is after-the-fact." Quests not jobs. Care *for* versus care *about*
([caring](https://myea.blog/caring)). The kindness close: "I'm not saying we won't die —
we might die. But... I will have been kind to those who tried"
([will-prevail](https://myea.blog/will-prevail)).

**Rhetorical questions.** He piles them to build pressure: "Why not? What would it take?
What special power will you acquire in the future that will make your reply better than
the reply you'd write now?" ([do-it-now](https://myea.blog/do-it-now)). "can kindness and
innocence survive the cruelty and indifference of our everyday world?"
([will-prevail](https://myea.blog/will-prevail)).

**Moral conviction.** This is his engine, not a tactic he reaches for. "I think aiming
high is morally correct" ([my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)). "The emotions of other
people should compel me just as much as my own" ([caring](https://myea.blog/caring)).
*Overfit watch:* conviction tips into contempt when it turns on people — "a bunch of you
are absolute morons" ([violent-passion](https://myea.blog/violent-passion)). Same
voltage, but it repels where the conviction alone would pull. (Already flagged in
[alejo-style](alejo-style.md).)

**High goals + confidence they're reachable.** He sets the goal enormous and then says
he'll get there. "AI will bring a century in a decade"
([ev-app](https://myea.blog/ev-app)); "Whatever it is, I will be able to solve it once I
figure out what it is" (same post). "founding another cG, running it for 10 years, and
then renouncing the CEO role to go take the role you were rejected for"
([violent-passion](https://myea.blog/violent-passion)). Antonakis pairs these two as the
core of inspirational tactics; Alejo runs them together by instinct.

**Reflecting the group's sentiment.** He does voice the room — but usually to push it: "I
want us to go back to the pre-FTX levels of ambition"
([my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)). He names what EAs feel, then tells them it's not
enough. That's a legitimate version; it's worth knowing it's the confrontational one, not
the consoling one.

**Three-part lists.** Present, but he runs past three into a rolling *and... and... and*:
"the joy, and the expansiveness, and the absolute thrill"
([colourful-heart](https://myea.blog/colourful-heart)) is a clean three; "apples and
branches and my trunk and my stump" ([giving-tree](https://myea.blog/giving-tree)) is
four and deliberately breathless. The triad is a tighter tool when the landing should be
crisp rather than flooding.

## Warmth — the constant

He runs every warmth signal the note names, without labeling them. **Reflecting sentiment
and handing over the ask:** "here's the thing I want from you, you don't have to give it
to me" ([avoidant](https://myea.blog/avoidant)) — the Maya frame, the calibrated,
lowest-cost version of his candor. **Naming the specific thing he noticed:** "I love that
you made couscous because you noticed I would like it"
([colourful-heart](https://myea.blog/colourful-heart)). **Wanting to be received:** "A
warm listener... where I feel like I can just pour my mind into them"
([my-manager](https://myea.blog/my-manager)). Warmth isn't a technique for him; it's the
default channel everything else rides on.

## The pratfall — his signature, and where he overfits

The note's version: establish competence, *then* let one honest flaw show — that converts
"impressive" into "endearing." He does this constantly: "a very perceptive friend recently
commented that I have absolutely zero common sense. I think he's onto something"
([my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)); "One [non-pure motivation] for me is my ginormous
pride" ([so-candid](https://myea.blog/so-candid)).

This is the one to watch hardest. The pratfall works as *one* flaw against *shown*
competence. His radical candor sprays many flaws at once — zero common sense, low
cognitive empathy, the paternalism screw-up, the pride — and a stack of confessions can
spend the competence the move depends on, tipping endearing into self-flagellating. The
guard isn't "confess less"; it's *let the competence land first, and let one flaw show at
a time.* That's the difference between McCartney choking up once over Lennon and a man
listing everything wrong with him.

## State — contagion, both ways

He propagates his state on purpose and knows it: "this intense almost manic energy that
has served me really well to convince people that they're worth it and they should try
hard" ([ev-app](https://myea.blog/ev-app)). "I surprised myself... how much conviction I
was able to convey, to the people closest to a recently departed friend, that things will
be good" ([will-prevail](https://myea.blog/will-prevail)).
*Overfit watch:* contagion has no off switch and runs both directions. The manic high is
catching; so is the depressive aftermath the [charisma note](charisma.md) and his own
[bipolar notes](../mind) describe. The state he spreads is the state he's in.

## Nonverbal — mostly unreadable from text, but one real gesture

Voice and face can't be judged from the blog. But he has a genuine signature gesture:
"When I do something cool and I notice it, I raise my hands above my heads and perform a
solo high-five" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)). That's his
thumbs-aloft — an unguarded, slightly silly, self-delighted move, the same *family* as
McCartney's. "I often say hi to people on the street" (same post) is the warmth-first
reflex in physical form.

## The one he doesn't do by default: presence

Every other component is already his. **Presence — full, undivided attention on one
person — is the exception, and he's said so:** "I'll shape my cognition to whatever shape
is most conducive to getting output from AIs... a lot of attention fragmentation"
([identity-barrier](https://myea.blog/identity-barrier)).

It's not absent — it's not his *default*. When care fires, he gives total attention: "In
one-on-ones it leads me to ask questions, be curious about what they hope for, what
they're trying to do" ([caring](https://myea.blog/caring)). So the raw capacity is there;
it's gated on caring and off by default. Since the note calls presence the most learnable,
highest-leverage component, and it's the only one he *doesn't* already run, it's the
single thing worth noticing — not a new skill to drill, just declining to fragment the
moments he's already in. This is the same conclusion [alejo-style](alejo-style.md) reached
from the other direction.
