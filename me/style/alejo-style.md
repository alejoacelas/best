# Alejo's style

A read of Alejo's own presence, in the same terms as the four profiles next door.
Sourced from [myea.blog](https://myea.blog). Read the [charisma note](charisma.md) first;
this applies its map to him. For the technique-by-technique version — which charisma
tactics he already runs, quoted from the blog — see [`alejo-techniques.md`](alejo-techniques.md).

## Thesis

Alejo's style is **radical self-exposure in the service of care**. He lays his interior
bare — motives, crushes, screw-ups, unflattering math — and does it *toward* people, to
move them. Warmth is the constant; candor is the engine; a hunt for hard rules is the
shape his thinking takes. The nearest of the four is McCartney (warmth + a real gift,
worn lightly), not Thiel — even though he borrows Thiel's provocations.

## The moves

- **Candor past the point of comfort.** The signature. He "persistently tr[ies] finding
  ways to be more candid than I've been in the past" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)),
  and the stated rule is [be so candid they ask you to stop](https://myea.blog/so-candid) —
  "just keep going until people literally start asking you to stop." At dinner he'll ask
  "You seem good at spotting exceptional talent, what's your take on me?" This is what the
  charisma note calls *accessible vulnerability*: he manufactures, deliberately and to an
  extreme, the status-lowering openness that makes McCartney endearing. It's his strongest
  charisma lever and his biggest liability at once.
- **Provocative true wording.** He "find[s] the most provocative true wording for what I
  think" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)) — "a bunch of you are
  absolute morons" ([violent-passion](https://myea.blog/violent-passion)), "may I steal
  this pen from you?" This is the Thiel move (say the disagreeable true thing), but he runs
  it warm, as an invitation, not a verdict.
- **Rule-distillation.** He "find[s] a small set of principles to follow religiously and
  then ignore most other considerations" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)):
  a meal is protein + carb + vegetable; [do things now](https://myea.blog/do-it-now);
  candor until they beg. When a trait misfires he doesn't resolve to try harder — he writes
  a rule ([my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)). The essays are him building his own operating
  manual in public.
- **Confession as structure.** He narrates his own worst moments and leaves the reasoning
  unresolved — the [schedule-send episode](https://myea.blog/my-bad), the [pride](https://myea.blog/pride)
  fueling him while a friend has just died. He shows the working, including the parts that
  don't tidy up. This is PG's think-out-loud, pushed to the emotional register PG never
  enters.
- **Sharp care.** The care he wants is "sharp, like a long stick or a sword, that can push
  through the crust and break things and shatter and reshape" ([caring](https://myea.blog/caring)) —
  opinionated, wanting to change the other person, the opposite of his grandparents'
  undirected blanket. Most of his warmth comes with a direction attached.
- **Direct address.** He writes letters — to Robin ([what-i-want](https://myea.blog/what-i-want),
  [colourful-heart](https://myea.blog/colourful-heart)), to a future manager
  ([my-manager](https://myea.blog/my-manager)), to the reader. Intimate second person; the
  reader is a specific person he's trying to reach.
- **Fiction as moral instrument.** Mother of Learning, HPMOR, Unsong, *Carrie*, the
  Giving Tree, Superman. He reasons about how to live through stories and cites them like
  evidence.
- **Speed over polish.** Dictated, bullet-shaped, published as drafts. The style *is*
  unfinished on purpose — [do it now](https://myea.blog/do-it-now), iterate in public.

## Where it comes from

Candor is an overcorrection he turned into a method. In his own words: "I used to restrict
myself much, much more... candor is kind of like my overcorrection" ([avoidant](https://myea.blog/avoidant)).
He pairs it with a stated **low cognitive empathy** — hard to read others' feelings — so
he leans on explicit rules and explicit disclosure *because* he can't reliably read the
room. That's the through-line: the rules, the candor, and the "harm only counts if you
tell me in words" stance are all the same adaptation to the same limitation, turned into a
recognizable voice.

## What it's for

- **Finding his people fast.** The blog's own aims: a long-term partner, collaborators,
  better conversations ([lay-bare](https://myea.blog/lay-bare)) — and by his account it's
  working. Candor is a filter: it repels the wrong people cheaply and bonds the right ones
  hard.
- **Attracting talent and mentors.** "An important mission pursued with conviction is one
  of the main factors for attracting talent. The other is being able to recognize the
  talent's strengths and create a narrative for how they fit" ([conviction](https://myea.blog/conviction)).
  The [manager post](https://myea.blog/my-manager) is this style used as a recruiting tool.
- **Moving people to act.** The day job is "proselytizing Claude Code" ([unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits))
  and coaching friends toward ambition ([violent-passion](https://myea.blog/violent-passion)).
  The intensity is the point — it's how he raises other people's aspirations.
- **Building trust that compounds.** [You can't compound on your own](https://myea.blog/cant-own):
  most of his leverage is other people, and being unusually easy and vivid to know is how
  he plugs into their networks.

## The costs

- **The candor has a body count.** [His pattern of disregarding people's immediate
  preferences](https://myea.blog/my-bad) — the schedule-send — strained someone he loves.
  Low cognitive empathy means he under-detects the harm his openness does, so his own alarm
  can't be trusted to stop him. The rule he landed on (default to their stated preference;
  override only after long thought) is the guardrail; the style needs it.
- **Provocation can read as contempt.** "Absolute morons" lands as bracing to some and as
  cruelty to others. Warmth has to be visibly present or the Thiel move curdles.
- **Earnestness with no irony is polarizing.** No cynical exit means people who don't want
  that intensity have nowhere to stand. That's fine as a filter; it's worth knowing it's a
  filter.
- **Speed costs compression.** [PG's density](people/paul-graham.md) is the thing his
  drafts most lack. Fast-and-bare is the voice; fast-and-*tight* would be strictly better.

## How to develop it

1. **Choose McCartney over Thiel, on purpose.** Alejo's gift is warmth, not menace. Keep
   the provocative true wording, but land it warm — the [Thiel profile](people/peter-thiel.md)
   shows what the cold version trades away, and it's not a trade he should want.
2. **Add PG's compression to the candor.** Same exposure, fewer words. Run drafts through
   the [density test](people/paul-graham.md) before publishing; the honesty survives
   cutting, the flab doesn't.
3. **Keep the guardrail explicit and public.** Candor without the [my-bad](https://myea.blog/my-bad)
   rule is the failure mode. Commit the rules where others can see him break them — that's
   the candor pointed back at himself.
4. **Borrow Tyler's curiosity as the warm form of provocation.** [Cowen](people/tyler-cowen.md)
   changes people by asking the question only he'd ask. For someone with low cognitive
   empathy, *asking* ("what's your take on me?") outsources the read he can't do — it's
   already his instinct; make it the default over asserting.
5. **Name the vulnerability instead of only enacting it.** The endearing quadrant needs
   accessible vulnerability, not raw oversharing. "Here's what I want, you don't have to
   give it" (the Maya frame, [avoidant](https://myea.blog/avoidant)) is the calibrated
   version — costs nothing, still fully open.

## Map to the four

- **McCartney** — closest. Warmth as default, a real gift worn lightly, openness that
  lowers status distance. Alejo's endearing quality and McCartney's are the same mechanism.
- **Graham** — shared: essay-as-thinking-out-loud, admitting uncertainty to earn trust,
  concrete-first. Missing: Graham's compression and calm.
- **Cowen** — shared: prolific, associative, curious, links everywhere, quests-not-jobs
  range. Missing: Cowen's deadpan restraint (Alejo runs hot where Cowen runs dry).
- **Thiel** — Alejo borrows the contrarian provocation and definite optimism, but he's
  Thiel's inverse on the axis that defines Thiel: Alejo badly wants to be liked, and he
  leads with warmth.

## Open

- Which of these is actually load-bearing for the good Alejo wants to do, versus just
  *him*? Candor and sharp care feel core; the provocation might be optional.
- The [charisma note](charisma.md) says presence is the most learnable, highest-leverage
  component. Alejo has all three — including **presence**, which he protects structurally:
  unscheduled, uninterrupted time so he can drop into something and just be there with
  people ([do-it-now](https://myea.blog/do-it-now), [unusual-habits](https://myea.blog/unusual-habits)).
  His attention only fragments toward *work with AI*; the open calendar is the wall that
  keeps that away from people. The thing to guard is that structure, not a new skill. See
  [alejo-techniques](alejo-techniques.md#presence--run-structurally-by-protecting-the-time-for-it).
