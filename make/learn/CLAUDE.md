Figuring out how the world works — the learning side of what I make.

## Layout
Flat: one folder per topic at the root (`agent-cli-dive`, `cowork-learn`,
`tag`, `base-rates`, …), each its own explorations and notes.
Top-level symlinks named for their topic point at wiki/reference writeups made
in other projects, gathered here so everything I've learned is browsable in one
place.
