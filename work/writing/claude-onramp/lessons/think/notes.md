Thinking is hard

engage with some thinks deeply. the ratio depends on the kind of work that you do, but there will be attributes (maybe career planning, strategy, charism, a specific area of expertise) where you really want to double down and think really hard, plenty of it for yourself

give it all the thinking you've already done, but be really wary of any friction, where you find yourself trying to do more thinking before handing to the AI. thinking is effortful and our brains would rather do less of it.

To get the most of AI, you have to answer a really hard question. What's the thinking you need to do *yourself* to achieve your goals.
