## Register note
Argument register wearing a confessional frame: the bracketed header sets it ("Epistemic status: very speculative… more a form of self-therapy"), and the evidence paragraphs read as symptom reports on his own feelings rather than citations. Compared to his argument-essay default, the model-building (Part I's cellular automata) is unusually front-loaded and the personal data unusually raw — the essay's authority comes from self-incrimination instead of sources.
