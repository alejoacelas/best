##  Working paper: key strategic considerations for taking action on AI welfare 

###  Eleos outlines the key strategic considerations that we consider in order to take near-term action on AI welfare while maintaining focus on long-term outcomes. 

AI companies and other decision-makers increasingly face decisions about the welfare and moral status of AI systems. In a new working paper by Head of Strategy Kathleen Finlinson, Eleos outlines key strategic considerations that guide near-term action on AI welfare while maintaining focus on long-term outcomes.
