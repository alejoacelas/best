# Effective altruism syllabi

First published: Apr 13, 2016 Last updated: Apr 16, 2026 222 words [effective altruism](/tags/effective-altruism/)

Table of contents

I’ve been helping some friends create a syllabus for a course on effective altruism, and as part of this effort I compiled a list of existing reading lists on the topic. Am I missing anything?

## Syllabi

  * [Luc Bovens & Stephan Chambers](https://www.lse.ac.uk/resources/calendar2019-2020/courseGuides/PH/2019_PH332.htm) (London School of Economics)
  * [Tom Bry-Chevalier & Antonin Broi](https://drive.google.com/file/d/11KR_9WqoTO-jvs8jHpTeK-_nbKpBctFc/view) (Paris Sciences et Lettres University)

  * [Stephen Casper](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1PO9nuEhVW4mDLleR8SkERJ3fIxJMUcYjWlBaVB9YbLE/edit) (Harvard University)

  * [Geoffrey Miller](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/Bd4xeHeNgBywrofW6/psychology-of-effective-altruism-course-syllabus-1) (University of New Mexico)
