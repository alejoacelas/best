##  Working paper: review of AI welfare interventions 

###  Recommendations for concrete action on AI welfare 

At Eleos AI, we are interested in better understanding potential AI sentience, moral patienthood, and welfare—and in making recommendations for concrete action. To make AI development go well, we not only need to improve our assessments of AI welfare, we also need to devise and evaluate interventions to protect and promote (potential) AI welfare. This working paper reviews several AI welfare interventions that have recently been proposed.

The working paper can be found [here](/papers/20250314_Preliminary_Review_of_AI_Welfare_Interventions.pdf).
