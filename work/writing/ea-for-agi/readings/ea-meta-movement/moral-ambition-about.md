# We are A movement of ambitious idealists

The School for Moral Ambition is a foundation and a growing movement. We help talented people move toward work that matters — taking on neglected problems like factory farming, Big Tobacco, and unfair tax systems.

The School for Moral Ambition emerged from a desire not only to talk about a better world but also to take action towards one.   
  
Our founders are writer [**Rutger Bregman**](https://rutgerbregman.com/nl), designer [**Harald Dunnink**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/haralddunnink/), former banker [**Julia van Boven**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/julia-van-boven/), former consultant [**Jan-Willem van Putten‍**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jan-willemvanputten/) and entrepreneur [**Ruben Timmerman**](https://ruben.org/).  
  
Our growing [team](/team) worked hard at setting up the [**Moral Ambition Circles**](/circles) program, in order to ensure moral ambition is an ideal everyone can aspire to. At the same time, we launched our **Moral Ambition Fellowships** in September of 2024.
