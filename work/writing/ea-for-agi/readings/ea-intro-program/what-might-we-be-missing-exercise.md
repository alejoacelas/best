If you're reading this, it probably means you've covered a lot of content! We hope you found it an interesting and enjoyable experience. 

There are many factors to account for when you try to do the most good you can, and lots of these ideas were likely new to you. (They were new to all of us at some point, too!) In this exercise, we ask you to reflect on previous content — what you found useful, and what you still feel skeptical about.
