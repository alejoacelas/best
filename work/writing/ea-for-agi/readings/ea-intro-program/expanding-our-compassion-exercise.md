This sequence includes two interactive exercises. You might find them interesting if you have the time, but don't feel obligated to complete them.

In the [**first exercise**](https://www.guidedtrack.com/programs/lvty83e/run), you'll consider how you weigh the welfare of different types of beings, and how that might affect your choice of where to donate. 

In the [**second exercise**](https://www.guidedtrack.com/programs/7q00bvi/run), you'll write a letter to a historical counterpart, trying to convince them to expand their own compassion. You'll also consider what a *future* counterpart might write in a similar letter, sent to... you.
